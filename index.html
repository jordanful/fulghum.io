Projects

  • TinyChart makes sharing data simple: paste CSV, auto-generate a chart, and share a link instantly with no account required.
  • Chapter is an open-source, self-hosted ebook reader with local AI voice narration. Seamlessly flip between reading and listening, with progress saved across modes and devices.
  • Bookend is the world's first AI assistant for the end of the world. An offline, local, one-click install chatbot that fits on a $10 thumb drive and is designed to help respawn civilization if SHTF.
  • VocalMaze, the first website that can give an accurate screening for cognitive decline to anyone in the world in under 60 seconds using a simple voice transcription and a transformer-based AI model that I fine-tuned.
  • I am always building other LLM/AI tools and toys, such as Promptspot (an open-source testing suite), Promptprompt (lightning-fast CDN prompt hosting), Gait (AI-powered Git CLI), and Echo (AI-generated podcasts before they were cool).
  • All Aboard, the world's best Chicago transit tracking app since 2012.
